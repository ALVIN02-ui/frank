A little about me

Hi, I’m Frank, a passionate tattoo artist and body piercer with 15 years of experience in the industry. I’ve honed my craft in various styles, from intricate black and grey work to bold, colorful designs. For me, tattooing is not just about creating beautiful images it's about telling a story, capturing a moment, and helping people express their unique identities.

I do amazing things for clients

Tattoo Artist

My expertise spans various styles—from intricate black and grey realism to vibrant, full-color designs—each crafted with meticulous attention to detail. Transforming ideas into exceptional, lifelong art with precision and passion.

Body Piercer

Expertly enhancing self-expression with precise, artistic body piercings.

TATTOO ARTIST Tutor

Guiding aspiring artists with patience and expertise, I’m dedicated to teaching the craft of tattooing with a focus on technique, creativity, and professionalism, ensuring that each student gains the confidence and skills needed to succeed in the industry.

High quality tattoo designs

Specializing in high-quality tattoos, I combine precision, creativity, and years of expertise to craft stunning, lasting art that my clients can proudly wear for a lifetime. Every piece I create is a testament to my commitment to excellence and artistic integrity.
